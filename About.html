About us!

The Banned Books Club began in April 2024 as a club on the website TrevorSpace dedicated to promoting community, inclusivity, and diversity in reading. Since then, the Banned Books Club has grown to include more than 225 members. This world is an interesting one ... and a lot of good books with queer representation (or any representation) are banned because some people can't see in front of their own noses. Well, welcome fellow world-beyond-nose-seers, and let's enjoy some of the best "banned" books of our time!
